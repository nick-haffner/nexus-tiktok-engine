# Level 4 Insight Brief

**Period:** All-time (45 posts) | **Generated:** 2026-04-03

---

## Cadence Assessment

The account averages **1.6 posts/week** — below the industry-standard 3-5/week for growth-phase accounts. The frequency-performance correlation is **negative**: higher-frequency weeks show lower per-post views. This is confounded by account maturation — high-frequency weeks are mostly early-era content (Sep-Nov 2025) when reach was minimal, while low-frequency weeks contain breakout posts. The correlation likely reflects growth, not a causal relationship.

## The 2-3 Day Sweet Spot

Posts preceded by a 2-3 day gap average **15.7K views** (n=14, high confidence), compared to 2.6K for 0-1 day gaps (n=16) and 438 for 4-7 day gaps (n=7). The 8+ day bucket averages 5.2K (n=7), inflated by a few strong posts following long breaks. The 2-3 day signal is the strongest timing finding — it suggests a rhythm of roughly 3 posts/week with deliberate spacing rather than daily bursts.

## Day-of-Week Patterns

Friday leads at 15.6K mean views (n=6, moderate confidence), followed by Wednesday at 10.9K (n=8). Sunday is a clear underperformer at 265 views (n=3, low confidence). Tuesday has the most posts (n=10) but a declining trajectory. Sample sizes are moderate — a single outlier could shift the ranking.

## Posting Pattern

The account is **bursty** (gap standard deviation: 7.3 days), alternating between dense stretches and multi-week silences. Consistent cadence correlates with higher views. This aligns with the 2-3 day gap finding: the algorithm appears to reward predictable rhythm over erratic volume.
