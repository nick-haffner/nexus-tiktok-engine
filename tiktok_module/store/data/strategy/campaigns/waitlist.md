# Campaign: Waitlist Launch

**Status:** Active  
**Goal:** Drive waitlist signups for Nexus travel recommendations app  
**Landing page:** nexus-concierge.com/waitlist  
**Bio link:** Must point to the landing page when this campaign is active

## Funnel Position

This campaign's TikTok organic posts are **Stage 1 (Awareness)** touchpoints. They introduce Nexus to cold viewers through value-first content, with the CTA converting interest into a waitlist signup.

See `strategy/marketing-funnel/funnel.yaml` for the full funnel definition and `utm_module/registry.csv` (row `606bc4f3`) for UTM parameters.

## CTA Slide Guidance

**Value prop:** The viewer just received local travel recommendations for a specific city. The CTA connects that experience to the product: an app that gives them local recs for every city they visit.

**Copy direction:**
- Bridge from the content: "Want recs like these for every city?"
- State what they get: local recommendations from real locals, for 30+ cities
- Action: "Link in bio" (primary), `nexus-concierge.com` (small reinforcing text)

**Caption CTA:** The caption should reinforce the waitlist ask since the slides stay editorial. Include the landing page URL in the caption body and frame it around early access or being first to get the app.

## What NOT to Do

- Don't say "join our waitlist" without explaining what the product is
- Don't make the URL the primary action — "link in bio" is the action, the URL is backup
- Don't use Nexus branding/logo on the CTA slide (see `../cta-strategy.md`)
