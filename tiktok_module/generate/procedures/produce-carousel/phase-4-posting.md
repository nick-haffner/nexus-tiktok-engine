## Step 4.1 — Generate the ChatGPT Caption Prompt

Claude generates a ready-to-paste prompt for the manager to use in ChatGPT. The prompt should include:

- The full approved slide text from `copy.md`.
- The city and framework context.
- Any meta-tips or advice that didn't fit on the slides (e.g., traffic timing, pro tips) — these are ideal caption content.
- Instructions for ChatGPT to produce:
  - **Title:** Short, attention-grabbing. This appears in the TikTok post listing — it is separate from the hook slide text.
  - **Caption:** At least 2-3 sentences. Longer captions help with the TikTok algorithm. Must include emojis (sparkles, stars, or context-appropriate like ☕ for coffee, 🌴 for a beach city). Should sell the carousel, encourage saves/shares, and include any bonus tips that didn't make the slides. Must include a CTA reinforcing the waitlist/product (e.g., "Join the waitlist at nexus-concierge.com" or "We're building your local friend in every city — sign up at nexus-concierge.com"). The caption is the primary conversion driver since the slides stay editorial. Front-load the hook text in the first 80-100 characters (this is what shows before "...more"). Hashtags go at the end of the caption, separated by a line break — never inline.
  - **Hashtags:** TikTok captions have a 4,000-character limit; hashtags count toward it. Best practice for conversion-focused posts is **3-5 hashtags** (TikTok's own Creator Portal recommends 3-5). More than that dilutes algorithmic signal and looks spammy. Use a **1:2 or 1:3 ratio of broad to niche** — e.g., 1 broad tag (#travel) paired with 2-3 city-specific or intent-specific tags (#losangelestips, #localtravelguide). Niche hashtags drive higher-intent traffic and better conversion. Avoid #fyp (no longer provides meaningful boost).
  - **Process:** Generate 5-10 candidate hashtags, then narrow to the 3-5 that best align with the intended audience while providing a mix of discoverability and conversion intent. Show the full list and the final selection with reasoning.
- Instruct ChatGPT to generate a single best recommendation with all three sections (title, caption, hashtags) — not multiple options.

Tell the manager: *"Here is the prompt to paste into ChatGPT to generate your title, caption, and hashtags. Review the output, request revisions in ChatGPT if needed, and save the final version to `caption.md` in the carousel directory. Then proceed to posting."*

> **MANAGER ACTION:** Copy the ChatGPT prompt. Open ChatGPT, paste it, review the output, revise if needed. Save the final title/caption/hashtags to `caption.md` in the carousel directory.
