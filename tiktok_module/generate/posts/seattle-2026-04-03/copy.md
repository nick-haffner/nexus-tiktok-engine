## Slide 2 — Food

- **Tourist:** Pike Place Chowder — Long lines, tourist prices, and it's fine — just fine.
- **Local:** Dick's Drive-In — Cash only. No substitutions. Seattle's been obsessed since 1954.

## Slide 3 — Views

- **Tourist:** Columbia Center Sky View — $25 to see the skyline from behind tinted glass
- **Local:** Alki Point Lighthouse — Free. Puget Sound, Olympic Mountains, and maybe three other people.

## Slide 4 — Things to See

- **Tourist:** The Gum Wall — It's exactly what it sounds like. An alley. Covered in gum.
- **Local:** The Fremont Troll — 18-ft concrete troll crushing a real VW Bug under a bridge.
