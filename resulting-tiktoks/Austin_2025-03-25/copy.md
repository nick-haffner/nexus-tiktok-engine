# Local vs Tourist in Austin

## Slide 1 — Hook
> Local vs Tourist in Austin

## Slide 2 — BBQ
- **Tourist Move:** Franklin BBQ. Great food, but the wait is brutal.
- **Local Move:** InterStellar BBQ. Michelin Star. No reservations, everyone is equal.

## Slide 3 — Nightlife
- **Tourist Move:** Dirty 6th Street. Overcrowded, messy, and totally overrated.
- **Local Move:** East 6th Street. Better cocktails, indie vibe, actually enjoyable.

## Slide 4 — Views & Sunsets
- **Tourist Move:** Mount Bonnell. Crowded stairs just to look at houses.
- **Local Move:** Congress Bridge Bats. The truly weird, iconic Austin sunset.

## Slide 5 — Chill / Outdoors
- **Tourist Move:** Downtown hotel pools. Overpriced, packed with bachelorette parties.
- **Local Move:** Barton Springs Pool. The classic, spring-fed local chill spot.

## Slide 6 — CTA
> Travel like a local. Read more in the caption.
