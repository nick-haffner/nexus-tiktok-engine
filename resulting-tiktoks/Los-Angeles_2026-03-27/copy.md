# LA in 24 Hours — Wasted Day vs Local Recs

## Slide 1 — Hook
> LA in 24 Hours — Wasted Day vs Local Recs 🌴

## Slide 2 — 🌅 Morning (Wasted) ❌
> Hollywood Walk of Fame.
> Dirty stars, tip-grabbing characters, and souvenir shops. That's it.

## Slide 3 — 🌅 Morning (Local) ✅
> Drive up PCH → Santa Monica Pier.
> Coast views, real beach time. This is how LA starts.

## Slide 4 — ☀️ Afternoon (Wasted) ❌
> Venice Beach Boardwalk.
> Overcrowded, overpriced, overwhelming.

## Slide 5 — ☀️ Afternoon (Local) ✅
> Griffith Park.
> Best views of the city and the Hollywood Sign. Free. Uncrowded on weekdays.

## Slide 6 — 🌆 Evening (Wasted) ❌
> Beverly Hills / Rodeo Drive shopping.
> Overpriced window shopping. You'll drive through it (the best way to see it) anyway.

## Slide 7 — 🌆 Evening (Local) ✅
> Find an event.
> A show at the Hollywood Bowl or Rose Bowl. A movie at El Capitan or the Westwood Village Theater.

## Slide 8 — 🍽 Dinner (Wasted) ❌
> Pink's Hot Dogs.
> 2-hour line. For a hot dog.

## Slide 9 — 🍽 Dinner (Local) ✅
> Angelini Osteria.
> Authentic Italian. Local favorite. No tourist circus.

## Slide 10 — 🌙 Late Night (Wasted) ❌
> Sunset Strip.
> Overpriced, overhyped, traffic-choked. The rock era is over.

## Slide 11 — 🌙 Late Night (Local) ✅
> Go out in Hollywood or Santa Monica.
> Either way, start at Cabo Cantina (locations in both). Then bar hop for dancing.
