# Local vs Tourist in Dallas

## Slide 1 — Hook
> Local vs Tourist in Dallas 🤠

## Slide 2 — 🏙 Views & First Impressions
- **Tourist Move:** Reunion Tower ONLY. Cool view, but you're paying just to look.
- **Local Move:** Reunion Tower → then drinks elsewhere. Go for the skyline shot... then leave and actually experience Dallas.

## Slide 3 — 🌮 Tex-Mex
- **Tourist Move:** Random Tex-Mex chain downtown. Overpriced, crowded, and made for visitors.
- **Local Move:** Desperados (Greenville Ave). This is where Dallas locals actually eat Tex-Mex.

## Slide 4 — 🤠 'Texas Experience'
- **Tourist Move:** Staying only inside Dallas city limits. You're missing half the culture.
- **Local Move:** Fort Worth Stockyards. "This is the Texas people expect — and it delivers."
- **On-screen text:** Dallas trip without Fort Worth? ❌

## Slide 5 — 🍹 Day Drinking / Chill Dallas Energy
- **Tourist Move:** Random rooftop bar. Looks good on Instagram, that's it.
- **Local Move:** Katy Trail Ice House. "Walk, drink, hang. This is Dallas soc."

## Slide 6 — 🍰 Dessert Stop
- **Tourist Move:** Generic dessert spot near tourist areas. Fine... but forgettable.
- **Local Move:** Emporium Pies (Bishop Arts). This is dessert as the main event.

## Slide 7 — 🍖 BBQ
- **Tourist Move:** Whatever BBQ is closest to your hotel. Looks Texas. Tastes average.
- **Local Move:** Terry Black's OR Hutchins BBQ. "Yes there's a line. Yes it's worth it."

## Slide 8 — 🍸 Nightlife / Drinks
- **Tourist Move:** Overcrowded Deep Ellum bar hopping. Loud, chaotic, and hit-or-miss.
- **Local Move:** Harwood Arms. Stylish, social, and actually enjoyable.
