# Local vs Tourist spots — SEATTLE

## Slide 1 — Hook
> Local vs Tourist spots SEATTLE

## Slide 2 — ☕ Coffee (Tourist)
- **Tourist Move:** Original Starbucks. Long line, same coffee.

## Slide 3 — ☕ Coffee (Local)
- **Local Move:** Ballard & Fremont coffee shops. Better roasts, better atmosphere.

## Slide 4 — 🏪 Markets (Tourist)
- **Tourist Move:** Pike Place at noon. Crowded. Stressful. Overrated.

## Slide 5 — 🏪 Markets (Local)
- **Local Move:** Pike Place early morning. Local vibes, real vendors, no crowds.

## Slide 6 — 🌇 Views (Tourist)
- **Tourist Move:** Space Needle restaurant. Paying for the view, not the food.

## Slide 7 — 🌇 Views (Local)
- **Local Move:** Kerry Park or Gas Works Park. Free views. Sunset hits harder.

## Slide 8 — 🦐 Seafood (Tourist)
- **Tourist Move:** Chain seafood on the waterfront. Frozen fish, tourist prices.

## Slide 9 — 🦐 Seafood (Local)
- **Local Move:** Taylor Shellfish & local oyster bars. Fresh, simple, Seattle done right.
