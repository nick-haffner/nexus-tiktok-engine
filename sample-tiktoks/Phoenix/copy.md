## Slide 2 — 🥾 Hikes & Views
- **Tourist Move:** Camelback Mountain at 9-10am (crowded, parking nightmare).
- **Local Move:** South Mountain Park / Dobbins Lookout or Phoenix Mountain views, easier parking, more chill.

## Slide 3 — 🌃 City Vibes / Night Out
- **Tourist Move:** Chain bars in CityScape / random bar next to the arena.
- **Local Move:** Roosevelt Row Arts District — murals, indie bars, galleries, First Friday art walk.

## Slide 4 — 🌮 Food
- **Tourist Move:** Generic Tex-Mex chain in a mall.
- **Local Move:** Sonoran hot dog stand / truck — classic Phoenix street food (bacon-wrapped hot dog, beans, toppings).
