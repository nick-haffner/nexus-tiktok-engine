> Modern venue, great atmosphere

> BUT it's far from San Francisco

## Slide 6 — 🍜 Dinner pick: Chinatown
> One of the best "night vibes" areas
> Great food + walking streets

## Slide 7 — 🍸 Dinner pick: Comstock Saloon
> Classic SF energy
> Perfect for cocktails + a real old-school vibe
