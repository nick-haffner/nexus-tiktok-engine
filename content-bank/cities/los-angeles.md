## Notes

- **Traffic is the defining constraint in LA.** The 24-hour carousel caption covers this: go on a weekday, start at 11, plan an activity from 4-7:30 to dodge rush hour. This insight could anchor the "Right Time vs Wrong Time" comedy concept.
- **Griffith Park vs Hollywood Sign** — Both are in the same area. Griffith has better views and is less iconic; Hollywood Sign is more recognizable. Used Griffith for the 24-hour carousel; Hollywood Sign is available for future use.
- **Cabo Cantina** — Has locations in both Hollywood and Santa Monica. Good starting point for either nightlife area.
